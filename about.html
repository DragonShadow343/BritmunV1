About Us

Welcome to BRITMUN VIII, an educational conference for school students in Years 9-13. Our conference exposes students to global issues and teaches them valuable skills such as diplomacy and public speaking. We hope you will join us for this exciting event.

The Mission

Our MUN conference aims to empower students to debate global issues and improve their understanding of international relations and public speaking. By simulating diplomacy and incorporating inspiring themes, we strive to create a dynamic and exciting event that educates and empowers attendees.

The Vision

BRITMUN VIII aims to address neglected global issues through debate and resolution-making. We strive to create a highly influential conference that encourages leadership and knowledge gain. Our councils allow participants to tackle pressing issues under the theme 'The Water Under Our Bridge'.
